James Joyce Links

  • james joyce portal: Alternate arrangement of Barger's site.

Ulysses

  • Ulysses for Dummies: Lighthearted, extremely brief overview.

Bloomsday Centenary

Festivals commemorating the centenary of Bloomsday, on June 16th, 2004.

  • ReJoyce Dublin 2004: the official Bloomsday Centenary Festival.
  • 19th International James Joyce Symposium, Dublin
  • Wandering Rocks, Revolving Doors: Nineteen separate pieces of art, installed throughout Dublin.
